## Three Steps to Get Started

### Step 1: Create an Account

Creating a Paystack account is free and takes a few minutes:
- Business name
- Full name
- Email
- Phone number
- Business type

### Step 2: Submit Compliance Documents

Requirements vary by location and business type. Review within 48 hours. Once approved, you can start accepting payments.

### Step 3: Integrate with Paystack

Integration options:
- **No-code**: Payment links, invoices
- **Low-code**: Pre-built checkout
- **Pro-code**: Full API integration

## Features

- Automated recurring payments
- Customized invoices
- Digital receipts
- Multi-currency support
- Real-time reporting
