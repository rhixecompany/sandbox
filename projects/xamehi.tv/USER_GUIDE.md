# User Guide — Xamehi.tv

Welcome to Xamehi.tv! This guide covers how to use our movie streaming platform from an end-user perspective.

## Overview

Xamehi.tv is a full-featured movie website where you can discover, watch, review, and rate movies. The live application is available at [xamehi.tv](https://www.xamehi.tv/).

## Features

- **Movie Discovery**: Browse movies with a top movie carousel and pagination
- **Movie Search**: Find movies quickly using the search feature
- **Reviews & Ratings**: Read and write movie reviews
- **User Profiles**: Create a personal profile to track your favorite movies
- **Admin Dashboard**: Content management for administrators

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [xamehi.tv](https://www.xamehi.tv/)
2. Click on "Sign Up" or "Register"
3. Fill in your details (email, username, password)
4. Verify your email if required
5. Log in with your credentials

### Browsing Movies

1. **Home Page**: View featured movies in the carousel
2. **Browse**: Use pagination to explore the full movie catalog
3. **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific movies by title

### Viewing Movie Details

1. Click on any movie poster or title
2. View movie details including:
   - Synopsis and description
   - Cast and crew information
   - User ratings and reviews
   - Related movie suggestions

### Writing Reviews

1. Navigate to a movie's detail page
2. Scroll to the reviews section
3. Write your review and submit a rating
4. Your review will appear immediately

### Managing Your Profile

1. Click on your profile icon
2. View your movie history and preferences
3. Update your profile settings
4. Manage your saved movies list

## Troubleshooting

- **Videos not playing**: Check your internet connection and browser compatibility
- **Login issues**: Verify your credentials and check for account verification emails
- **Search not working**: Try refreshing the page or using different search terms
