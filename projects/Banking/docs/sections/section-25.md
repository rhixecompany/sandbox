# Section 25 — Plans & Change Management

- If a change touches more than 7 files, create a plan in `.opencode/commands/` before implementing.

Note: Legacy plan artifacts may exist under `.opencode/plans/` or `.cursor/plans/`. Preserve those files for historical provenance — do not move or delete them. Use `.opencode/commands/` for all new user-facing plan files.

- Plans should include Goals, Scope, Target Files, Risks, Planned Changes, Validation, Rollback.
