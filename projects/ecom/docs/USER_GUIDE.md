# User Guide

## Getting Started

Welcome to RhixeCompany E-Commerce! This guide will help you navigate and use the platform effectively.

## Account Management

### Creating an Account

1. Click **Register** in the top navigation
2. Enter your name, email address, and password
3. Click **Register** to create your account
4. You will be redirected to your profile page

### Logging In

1. Click **Login** in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**
4. You'll be redirected to your profile

### Profile Management

Once logged in, you can:
- View your account information
- Update your shipping address
- View order history
- Change your password

## Shopping

### Browsing Products

- **Home Page**: View featured products and the product carousel
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific products
- **Categories**: Browse products by category (filter not visible in UI)

### Adding Items to Cart

1. Click on any product to view details
2. Select quantity (if more than 1)
3. Click **Add to Cart**
4. Cart icon in header shows item count

### Managing Your Cart

- View cart by clicking the cart icon
- Adjust item quantities using +/- buttons
- Remove items by clicking the X button
- View order summary and totals

### Checkout Process

1. **Cart Review**: Verify items in your cart
2. **Shipping**: Enter shipping address
3. **Payment**: Select payment method (PayPal or credit card)
4. **Place Order**: Confirm and submit order

## Order Management

### Placing an Order

1. Add items to cart
2. Proceed to checkout
3. Enter shipping information
4. Select payment method
5. Confirm order

### Order History

- Log in to your account
- Navigate to **Profile**
- View all past orders in order history
- Click on any order to see details

### Order Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Processing** | Order received, being prepared |
| **Paid** | Payment confirmed |
| **Shipped** | Order is on its way |
| **Delivered** | Order received by customer |

## Admin Features

### Admin Access

Admins have access to:
- Product management (add, edit, delete)
- User management (view, edit)
- Order management (view, update status)
- Mark orders as delivered

### Managing Products

1. Log in as admin
2. Navigate to **Admin** section
3. Click **Products** to view all products
4. Use **Add Product** to create new products
5. Click **Edit** on any product to modify
6. Click **Delete** to remove products

### Managing Orders

1. Navigate to **Admin** > **Orders**
2. View all customer orders
3. Click on an order to see details
4. Click **Mark as Delivered** to update status

## Payment

### PayPal

1. Select PayPal at checkout
2. You will be redirected to PayPal
3. Log in and confirm payment
4. Return to the store automatically

### Credit Card

1. Select Credit Card at checkout
2. Enter card details (processed by PayPal)
3. Confirm payment

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot add to cart
- Ensure you are logged in
- Check that product is in stock

### Payment failed
- Verify card details
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Try alternative payment method

### Order not showing
- Check order history in profile
- Verify payment was processed
- Contact support

### Cannot login
- Verify email and password
- Check if account was created
- Use password reset option

## Support

For additional help:
- Check the [FAQ](#) (link to be added)
- Contact support via email
- Check order status in profile

## Tips

- Save your shipping address in your profile for faster checkout
- Check product reviews before purchasing
- Use search to quickly find products
