     1|# Blog Post Workflow
     2|
     3|## Timeline: 1-2 weeks (with feedback cycles)
     4|
     5|## Phase 1: Planning (30 min)
     6|
     7|**Step 1: Clarify the angle**
     8|
     9|- What's the core argument or insight you want to share?
    10|- Who are you writing for?
    11|- What problem does this post solve for them?
    12|- What will they know/feel differently after reading?
    13|
    14|**Step 2: Outline together**
    15|
    16|- Hook/opening (compelling question, story, or stat)
    17|- 3-4 main sections (building argument or teaching concept)
    18|- Conclusion (summary + call to action or next step)
    19|
    20|**Step 3: Identify research needs**
    21|
    22|- What data would strengthen this?
    23|- Any expert quotes or examples needed?
    24|- Sources you already have vs. need to find
    25|
    26|## Phase 2: Research (1-2 hours)
    27|
    28|**Step 1: Find sources**
    29|
    30|- Blog posts, studies, tweets, customer examples
    31|- Prioritize recent, credible sources
    32|- Lean on personal experience + research (not just links)
    33|
    34|**Step 2: Extract key findings**
    35|
    36|- 3-5 key points from research
    37|- Direct quotes if strong
    38|- Examples that illustrate your point
    39|
    40|**Step 3: Review together**
    41|
    42|- Confirm research supports your thesis
    43|- Discard anything that dilutes focus
    44|- Note where research fits in outline
    45|
    46|## Phase 3: Writing (2-3 hours spread across 3 days)
    47|
    48|**Day 1 — Hook + First Section:**
    49|
    50|- Write compelling hook/intro (150-200 words)
    51|- Complete first section (300-400 words)
    52|- Share for feedback
    53|
    54|**Feedback Cycle:**
    55|
    56|- I review for clarity, flow, and whether examples land
    57|- Suggest improvements, offer alternatives
    58|- You revise based on what resonates
    59|
    60|**Day 2 — Sections 2-3:**
    61|
    62|- Write sections 2 and 3 (300-400 words each)
    63|- Each section should build on previous insight
    64|- Share sections independently for targeted feedback
    65|
    66|**Day 3 — Conclusion + Polish:**
    67|
    68|- Write conclusion that summarizes + calls to action
    69|- Read entire draft aloud
    70|- Share for full review
    71|
    72|## Phase 4: Revise (1-2 hours)
    73|
    74|**Step 1: Review feedback**
    75|
    76|- Focus on structure and flow feedback first
    77|- Then tackle clarity and evidence gaps
    78|- Polish comes last
    79|
    80|**Step 2: Revise strategically**
    81|
    82|- Major issues first (structure, evidence)
    83|- Then clarity (word choice, flow)
    84|- Then style (polish, consistency)
    85|
    86|**Step 3: Final check**
    87|
    88|- Read aloud one more time
    89|- Check: hook strong? conclusion lands? CTА clear?
    90|- Verify all claims supported
    91|
    92|## Phase 5: Publish (1 hour)
    93|
    94|**Before publishing:**
    95|
    96|- [ ] Proofread for typos and grammar
    97|- [ ] Format: headers, bold, lists clear
    98|- [ ] All links working
    99|- [ ] Featured image ready
   100|- [ ] Meta description written
   101|
   102|**Publishing:**
   103|
   104|- Add to CMS/blog platform
   105|- Social media post draft
   106|- Internal sharing plan
   107|
   108|## Tips for Blog Success
   109|
   110|- **Voice matters** — Blog readers want authentic perspective, not corporate tone
   111|- **Examples are oxygen** — Generic claims bore; specific examples engage
   112|- **Link generously** — Show your sources; gives reader confidence + SEO benefit
   113|- **Call to action** — Don't leave readers hanging; tell them what's next
   114|- **Deadline helps** — "I want to publish this Thursday" creates focus
   115|
   116|## What Feedback Looks Like (Blog-Specific)
   117|
   118|**For blog posts, I focus on:**
   119|
   120|- Does the hook grab you in first 2 sentences?
   121|- Is the core argument clear by end of section 1?
   122|- Do examples feel specific and true?
   123|- Is the voice consistent and authentic?
   124|- Does conclusion drive action?
   125|- Is this shareable? Would you tweet this?
